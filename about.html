My Story

Hi, My name is Patrick Burke. I am originally from Chicago and have been based in New York City since 2013. I began my career in software design in 2020 at the beginning of the pandemic. Prior to that I had been working in restaurants and translation since I graduated from Northwestern. I have traveled to over forty countries and worked in the food industry in New York, Chicago, London, and Paris.

My love for travel developed a passion for connecting with people of all cultures, and I soon realized language was the most valuable key for those connections. I began studying foreign langauges with a genuine interest, and the more they enhanced my life, the more I dedicated myself to finding effective methods to utilize my time and produce results. I soon garnered a small collection of pupils that I could teach these methods to and check on their progress. One of the most useful tools for this dedication has been mobile apps.


As more people are looking to mobile apps to at least get their feet wet, they're quickly becoming an indispensable method to begin or continue one's pursuit of knowledge. I have used mobile applications to enrich my skills in language, music, and even coding, and I see this growing trend soon becoming a paradigm shift in education. I am looking to work for a company that shares my values in making education accessible, engaging, and effective. I am willing to work freelance or relocate worldwide. In addition to my interests in educaiton, I am a multi-intsrumentalist and yoga instructor.
